Burger

Description

This juicy homemade burger recipe features a tender beef patty, crisp lettuce, and a flavorful sauce, perfect for a weekend BBQ!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and melt some butter.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side until they reach your desired doneness.
  4. Toast the burger buns in the same skillet.
  5. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese, and the cooked beef patty.
  6. Add ketchup, mustard, and any other preferred condiments before serving.
