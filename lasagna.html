Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is full of rich flavors and cheesy goodness, perfect for a hearty family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic in a pan.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, cheese, and beef in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
