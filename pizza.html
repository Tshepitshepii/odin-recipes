Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe will give you a delicious, cheesy, and crispy pizza that’s better than takeout!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil
  • Basil leaves
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, followed by pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil before serving.
